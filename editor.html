About Filter Levels

Your filter includes 4 strictness levels you can switch between in-game using the filter level hotkey. No selection needed here — just press Next to continue.

Level 1 — Relaxed: Shows almost everything. Good for new characters and early leveling.

Level 2 — Normal: Hides low gold, junk white/magic items, scrolls, and keys. The default for most gameplay.

Level 3 — Strict: Hides exceptional whites, low-tier uniques/sets, and most rares. For experienced players farming specific content.

Level 4 — Very Strict: Only shows high-value items. Hides nearly all non-elite gear. For endgame map farming.

Note: All runes are always shown regardless of filter level.
